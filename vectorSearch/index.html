What Distance Means

This table is sorted by Cosine distance, which compares the angle/s of two vectors: your query and a given park's description.

A smaller vector "distance" can indicate similarity (i.e., a "semantic match"). In this example, a smaller number would indicate a more relevant park description, relative to your search query.
